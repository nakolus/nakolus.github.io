ME Box

This is a product concept for designing a small device. M represents mine, mind and music; E means extensive, emotion and electronics. Combining M with E, ME, is a small electronic music playing device capable of displaying various colored LED lights with rhythm while playing music. This device was designed as personal emotional outlet through the sense of hearing and vision with melody and colorful lights. Meanwhile, this device is equipped with a mini USB port for power charging, music display controlling, and LED lights settings.
