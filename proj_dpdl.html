DPDL

different perspective, different landscape

Different perspectives lead to different landscapes. I was inspired by Penrose Triangle designed by Oscar Reutersvärd. With the inspiration from impossible designs, I turned impossibility to possibility. Similar to changes in perspective, different interpretations come with changes in viewpoint. Through images, I rotated my camera trying to find possible visual angles in three-dimensional space and displayed their possibilities in two-dimensional picture though a sphere’s movement as shown in the picture. Often times, things can be incorrect in one perspective yet correct in others. Different does not equal incorrect. Impossibility can be a possibility.
