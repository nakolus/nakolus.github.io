Teapot dream

Teapot was an interesting exercise in learning computer graphics, for instance, Utah teapot. Teapot was an option in the default module when I first operated computer graphics software, 3ds Max. Due to its elegant curves, it was an excellent object for lighting and effects tests.

Teapot dream is a story inspired by teapot. There was a special teapot, a pretentious teapot. This teapot existed and danced in a higher position different from other teapots. And something happened on this special teapot. This is a fiction about a teapot.
