Guess Number

Parameters lead to numerous possibilities in creating computer graphics objects. Different settings in numbers work magically to produce outcomes. The process of guessing the magical numbers inspired the game of “Guess Number”. The instructions are as follows. The answer is a string of four non-repetitive numbers range from 0 to 9. The status of your guessing will be shown in recording fields. Meanwhile, you can click “cheat” to peep the correct answer.




Play Game




Enter four different numbers

( 0 ~ 9 )





Answer         Status











    “A” means the number exists in the correct answer and corresponds to the same order.

    “B” means the number exists in the correct answer but corresponds to a different order.

    For instance, if the answer was “1234,” the status would show “1A2B” when entering “1302”.

    You have ten attempts to guess the correct string of numbers.
